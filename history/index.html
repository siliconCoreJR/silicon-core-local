OUR HISTORY

25 Years of Innovation

From our founding in Silicon Valley to our status as the industry leader in fine pixel pitch LED display manufacturing, our story is one of continuous innovation and industry firsts.

2011

Industry Disruption

SiliconCore enters the market with the world's highest resolution LED displays and revolutionary Common Cathode technology, disrupting the display industry.

2013

Common Cathode Patented

SiliconCore receives patent for Common Cathode technology — delivering up to 40% energy savings over conventional LED displays.

2016

ZACH Technology

Introduction of ZACH image processing technology delivering exceptional high dynamic range and deeper blacks for unprecedented color uniformity.

2018

Sub-1mm Pixel Pitch

SiliconCore achieves sub-1mm pixel pitch LED displays, opening new markets in enterprise visualization, broadcast, and control rooms.

2020

Virtual Production

SiliconCore LED walls become the go-to choice for major film and broadcast virtual production studios worldwide.

2023

Enlighten COB Series

Launch of Enlighten — SiliconCore's COB Series delivering the highest performance dvLED displays with ultra-low power consumption.

2025

TRAK-KIT Partnership

SiliconCore partners with TRAK-KIT to introduce kinetic dvLED display systems that transform static spaces into dynamic visual environments.

The future of LED is brighter than ever. SiliconCore continues to invest in next-generation driver chip technology, ultra-fine pixel pitches, and sustainable display solutions.
