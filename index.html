The Technology

LISA Technology

LED In-Surface Application protects LEDs from damage with no compromise on brightness, maintaining life expectancy in rugged environments.

Common Cathode IQ

The only manufacturing process we use. Pixel pitches as low as 0.83mm, up to 40% less power, low operating temperatures and fan-less operation.

ZACH Technology

Embedded in our driver chips, ZACH achieves uniform brightness and homogenous color across the display — including near Rec.2020 color gamut.

Life-like color reproduction

Our LED display's 16 bit range achieves a vivid color gamut beyond industry standard to near Rec.2020 for life-like color reproduction.

The brightest LED displays

With a brightness capacity up to 40% higher than the industry average, consistent brightness levels can be maintained across the life of the product.

Cool & quiet operation

Our LED display operating temperatures run cool-to-the-touch, eliminating the requirement for fan cooling systems and reducing noise interruption.

Highest dynamic range

Greater color range for greater depth and uniformity — especially at lower brightness levels where most displays struggle to maintain quality.

Lower energy consumption

By reducing heat dissipation from within the display, the overall power consumption of our displays is reduced by 20–50% for a more sustainable investment.
