Patented Technologies That Set Us Apart

SiliconCore is the only dvLED manufacturer to design its own driver chips. This gives us unparalleled control over performance, power management, and reliability.

ZACH Technology

Exceptional high dynamic range and deeper blacks for better color uniformity. ZACH (Zero Ambient Contrast Handling) is our proprietary image processing technology that delivers stunning visual performance in any lighting condition.

Calibrated to within 1% color and brightness tolerance across the entire display surface.

Common Cathode IQ

As the industry originators of Common Cathode, we've built the next generation of driver chip technology with unmatched power management to each individual LED.

This delivers up to 40% energy savings, keeps displays cool-to-the-touch, and significantly extends operational lifetime to over 100,000 hours.

LISA Encapsulation

LISA (LED In-Surface Application) is our unique encapsulation method that lies LED crystals flat with black material and applies an optical coating on top to improve contrast and protect LED components.

The result is a display with superior black levels and dramatically enhanced contrast ratios.

Chip Design

We are the only dvLED manufacturer to design our own driver chips. Chip design is in our DNA. This unique capability gives us total control over power management, color accuracy, and performance optimization — ensuring every SiliconCore display delivers the best possible image quality with the lowest possible power consumption.
