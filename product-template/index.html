About the Series

Engineered for the most demanding environments

The Enlighten Signature series is SiliconCore's flagship indoor dvLED platform. Combining our proprietary LISA surface-mount technology with Common Cathode IQ and ZACH calibration, every cabinet delivers extraordinary uniformity and color accuracy straight out of the box.

Available in pixel pitches from 0.78mm to 1.9mm, Enlighten Signature scales from intimate boardrooms to expansive control rooms — all within a single, consistent cabinet ecosystem.

Fan-less. Cool-running. Built to outlast the competition.

Why Enlighten Signature

What sets this series apart

Common Cathode IQ

The only manufacturing process we use. Up to 40% less power, lower operating temperatures, and fan-less operation as a result.

LISA Surface Protection

LEDs encapsulated in our proprietary surface material — shielded from physical contact and ambient damage without any brightness trade-off.

ZACH Calibration

Factory-embedded per-pixel brightness and color uniformity. Near Rec.2020 color gamut, consistent across the full life of the display.

16-bit Greyscale

65,536 intensity levels versus the industry-standard 8-bit. Eliminates banding at low brightness and delivers smooth, natural gradients.

1,800 nit Peak Brightness

Enough headroom to perform in high-ambient-light environments with color fidelity fully maintained at every output level.

Modular Cabinet System

All pixel pitches share the same cabinet footprint, simplifying installation, spares management, and future expansion.
