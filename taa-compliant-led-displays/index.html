Trade Agreements Act Compliant LED Displays

SiliconCore offers a range of TAA compliant LED display solutions for U.S. government and federally funded projects. Our TAA compliant products meet all requirements under the Trade Agreements Act, ensuring your project qualifies for government procurement programs.

Why TAA Compliance Matters

The Trade Agreements Act (TAA) requires that products purchased by the U.S. government are manufactured or substantially transformed in a TAA-designated country. SiliconCore's TAA compliant displays are designed and manufactured to meet these strict requirements.
